# 🏥 Patient–Doctor Coordination System  
*A Comprehensive Feature Blueprint*

---

## 🌟 Overview

This document outlines a **comprehensive patient–doctor coordination system** designed to enhance communication, monitoring, and personalized treatment through structured dashboards, secure messaging, and analytics-driven insights.

---

## 🧑‍⚕️ Core Features for Patient–Doctor Coordination

---

## 1️⃣ Patient Side Features

### A. View Assigned Doctor
- Display doctor’s **name**, **specialization**, and **institution**
- Show **contact information** (if shared)
- Display **assignment date** and **treatment goals**
- Option to **request doctor assignment** if none is assigned

---

### B. Doctor Dashboard Widget
- Quick view of **current assigned doctor**
- **Last check-in date**
- **Upcoming appointments/reviews**

---

### C. Request Doctor Assignment
- Allow patients to request assignment to a **specific doctor** (with consent)
- Submit **reason for requesting doctor oversight**

---

### D. Share Progress with Doctor
- Toggle to **enable/disable data sharing**
- **Privacy & consent management**
- Selectable data sharing options:
  - Baseline assessments  
  - Training results  
  - Progress metrics  

---

## 2️⃣ Doctor Side Features

### A. Patient Management
- View **all assigned patients**
- **Search & filter** patients
- **Assign / unassign** patients
- Set **diagnosis** and **treatment goals** per patient

---

### B. Patient Detail View
- Complete **patient profile**
- **Baseline assessment results**
- **Training history** and progress charts
- **Performance trends** over time
- Recent **activity timeline**

---

### C. Interventions & Recommendations
- Add **clinical notes / observations**
- Recommend **specific training tasks**
- Modify **difficulty levels**
- Set **performance goals**
- Schedule **check-ins**

---

### D. Progress Monitoring
- Compare **baseline vs current performance**
- Detect **concerning trends** (e.g., decline)
- Track **adherence** (training frequency)
- Monitor **domain-specific improvements**

---

## 3️⃣ Communication Features

### A. Secure Messaging
- Doctor ↔ Patient **secure chat**
- Question & response workflow
- **Message history** with threading
- **Read receipts**

---

### B. Alerts & Notifications

#### Doctor Receives Alerts When:
- Patient hasn’t trained in **X days**
- Significant **performance drop**
- Patient completes **baseline assessment**
- Patient **requests help**

#### Patient Receives Notifications When:
- Doctor sends a **message**
- Doctor adds **new recommendations**
- **Scheduled review** is approaching

---

## 4️⃣ Clinical Tools

### A. Progress Reports
- Auto-generated **weekly / monthly reports**
- Export data as **PDF / CSV**
- Visual **charts & graphs**
- Doctor **commentary section**

---

### B. Treatment Plans
- Customize **training plans**
- Define **cognitive domain focus areas**
- Adjust **task difficulty parameters**
- Track **plan effectiveness**

---

### C. Clinical Notes
- **Time-stamped** patient notes
- Tag notes by category:
  - Observation  
  - Concern  
  - Improvement  
- **Private notes** (doctor-only)
- **Shared feedback** (visible to patient)

---

## 5️⃣ Analytics Dashboard

### A. Doctor Analytics
- Overview of **all patients**
- **Overall adherence rate**
- Cohort-level **success metrics**
- Identify **high-risk patients** needing attention

---

### B. Patient Analytics
- View progress from **doctor’s perspective**
- Understand personal **cognitive profile**
- Increased motivation through **doctor engagement**

---

## ✅ Outcome

This system creates a **closed-loop digital healthcare experience**—enhancing transparency, accountability, clinical oversight, and patient motivation through structured coordination and data-driven decision-making.
